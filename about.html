About Read To Excel

A cradle of excellence and godliness, raising well-rounded leaders in Akure, Ondo State.

Who we are

A community built on excellence and godliness

Read To Excel International School (RTEI) is a private school in Akure, Ondo State, offering a complete learning pathway from Creche through College. Since our beginning, we have focused on one purpose: raising well-rounded leaders for a global world.

We pair qualified, caring teachers with a safe, well-equipped environment so that every child can thrive academically, morally and spiritually.

Our Mission

We are committed to raising well-rounded leaders through quality education anchored on excellence and godliness.

Our Vision

To be a leading school recognised for raising confident, godly leaders equipped to compete globally.

Our Motto

Cradle of Excellence and Godliness — the spirit that guides everything we do.

At Read To Excel, we don't just teach children to pass exams — we raise leaders of character who carry excellence and godliness into the world.

— The Proprietor, Read To Excel International School
