Our learning pathway

A continuous journey from early care to secondary education — each stage building character and competence.

Early Years

Creche

Warm, attentive care for our youngest learners in a safe, loving setting that builds security and trust.

Foundation

Kindergarten (KG)

Play-based foundation years that grow curiosity, language, early numeracy and social confidence.

Primary

Basic

A strong primary education built on literacy, numeracy, inquiry and good character.

Secondary

College

Rigorous secondary studies that prepare confident students for national exams and the wider world.

Enquire
